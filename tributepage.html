President Umar Musa Yaradua

The president with the greatest ambition for Nigerians

Umaru Musa Yar'Adua was the 13th president of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. He was the governor of Katsina State in northern Nigeria from 29 May,1999 to 28 May,2007. He was declared the winner of the controversial Nigerian presidential election held on 21 April,2007, and was sworn in on 29 May,2007


Here's a time line of Umaru Musa Yar'Adua's life:

  • Born: 16 August 1951, Katsina State

  • Education: Ahmadu Bello University (1972–1975), Barewa College (1971), Ahmadu Bello University

  • Awards:CAF Platinum Award

  • Spouse: Umar Radda (m. 1992–1997), Turai Yar'Adua (m. 1975–2010)

  • Children: Zainab Yar'Adua Dakingari, Nafisat Yar'Adua Yuguda

  • Previous office: President of Nigeria (2007–2010)

  • Died: 5 May 2010, Aso Villa, Abuja
